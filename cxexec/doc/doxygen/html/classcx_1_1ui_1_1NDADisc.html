Detailed Description

Connect X disc for the next disc area.

This is a special variant of the Connect X disc specialized to behave according to the requirements of the next disc area. More specifically:

  • it has no background (i.e. transparent);
  • when hidden, the disc's fill area and border become transparent also.

in other words, when such a disc is considered hidden, it becomes completely invisible to the user.
