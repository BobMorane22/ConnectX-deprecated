Detailed Description

A utility to manage Players of a Disc related game.

A Player object is a Name and Disc pair.

Invariant
A Player's Disc cannot have NO_COLOR and the space (' ') character as their Color attributes. In other words, a Player cannot possess a NO_COLOR Disc, which is seen as no disc.
